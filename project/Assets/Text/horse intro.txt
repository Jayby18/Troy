The end of the war came with Odysseus' world famous plan:
a giant hollow wooden horse filled with Greek soldier,
who could infiltrate Troy and could attack the Trojans when they're asleep.
The horse had the following inscription:
"The Greeks dedicate this thank-offering to Athena for their return home."
The Trojans were happy with it.
Thinking the Greeks were gone, they pulled the horse in.
But there was one Trojan who felt that this wasn't right...
